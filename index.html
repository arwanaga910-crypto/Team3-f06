colombian coffee

Flavor: Balanced between sweetness and bitterness, with hints of nuts or chocolate. Aroma: Rich and aromatic, sometimes with dried fruit notes. Body: Medium, smooth on the palate.

Acidity: Mild and pleasant, not too sharp. Origin: Grown in the high mountains of Colombia, known for ideal coffee climate.

Flavor: Strong and slightly bitter, with dark chocolate or caramel notes. Aroma: Rich and roasted. Body: Heavy and full-bodied.

Acidity: Low, focusing more on bitterness and depth. Origin: Traditionally roasted and brewed in Italy for a bold, intense shot.
